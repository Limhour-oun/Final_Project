Welcome to College of Science and Technology of The University of Cambodia. For everyone who wishes to pursue for any AA and BA degrees of the study programs, please register now or visit the campus of The University of Cambodia.

Electronics and telecommunications engineering is a modern engineering discipline that deals with designing, fabricating, producing, testing and supervising the manufacturing process of complex electronic products and systems.

Student Outcomes

Our learning outcomes are statements that describe the skills we expect students to attain by the time of graduation.

1 - Students master the methodology for conducting research, have critical thinking and creativity; have the ability of self-learning and studying electronics and telecommunications at the graduate level.
2 - Students are able to design and implement electronic equipment, operate telecommunication systems, and manage, consult, and provide technical support on electronic and telecommunication products and projects.
3 - Students have the ability to work independently as well as in groups under high-pressure environments; they can develop and integrate into high-quality job markets.
4 - Students are trained to be electronic and telecommunication engineers with strong specialized knowledge, good health, and professional ethics.
